About Us

Reconnecting the KNUST Community

The KNUST Lost & Found platform is a web-based solution developed by a team of ten students as part of a Web Development project at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology.

This platform was created to address a common challenge on campus — the loss and misplacement of personal belongings. From student IDs and wallets to phones and academic materials, losing an item can cause unnecessary stress and disruption.

Our goal is to provide a centralized, secure, and user-friendly system where students and staff can report, search for, and recover lost items efficiently.

Our Purpose

We aim to:

  • Create a structured digital system for reporting lost and found items
  • Reduce the stress associated with losing valuables
  • Promote honesty, responsibility, and community trust
  • Make the recovery process faster and more reliable

How it Works

  1. Report a lost or found item through the platform.
  2. Provide clear details and relevant descriptions.
  3. Browse listings to find potential matches.
  4. Verify ownership before items are returned.

Our goal is to make the process straightforward and secure for all users.
